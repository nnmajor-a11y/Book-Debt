Welcome Booklovers

Does This Sound Familiar?

The bookshelves are piled to the max with many unread books or books that haven't been read in a long while. The To-Read List continues to grow faster and faster, but never shorter and shorter. Whether this continuously growing collection of books came from gifts, personal shopping, or others, there are more untouched books than read books. But you can't help yourself!


Weither you walk into the bookstore, the tranquil atmosphere, beautiful book covers, and intoxicating smells from the in-store cafe makes you want to buy another book and join in with your fellow booklovers in the happiest place on earth. Or, the recommendations from BookTok, freinds and family are just too inticing to ignore. You just have to buy that new recommendation now, even though you have a few books you promised yourself you'd read before making another purchase. Or, you bought one book from the series, you might as well buy the whole set!

Either Way, You Are In Debt!

At least to yourself and your books.

But, here's something that can help.

Introducing Book Debt!

A Tracker and A Budgeter for Books

Book Debt is your ready-made Excel sheet that:

  • Keeps track of how many books you bought.
  • Keep track of how many of the books.
  • Calculate how many new books you are allowed to buy on your next book haul.
  • Calculate how many currently owned books you have to read before buying more books
